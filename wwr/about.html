Dry Oar Adventures

Come for the thrill of a lifetime. If you are looking for an adventure to top all adventures, white water river rafting with Dry Oar will not disappoint. Our professional guides know the river and how to navigate it for the best experience. Come with a group or by yourself. You will meet others in it for the adventure. We provide everything you need to have a most memorable experience. We have outfits on five different rivers throughout the Rocky Mountains.

History

Dry Oar Adventures started running the Colorado River in 1985 with one raft. Over the past 40 years we have been offering a lifetime experience to thousands of adventures. Over the years we have added new locations to expand the thrill of white-water rafting to more of you.

We now run five different rivers throughout the Rocky Mountains. We have a staff of experienced guides who have an extensive knowledge of the rivers they run. We also have our ground crews who provide the logistics to get you to the river and back as well as make your experience memorable.
